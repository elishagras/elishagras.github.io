Melodease: Personalized Music Practice Tracking Platform

← Back to Portfolio

Case Study

Melodease is an adaptive music education platform for students of all skill levels - blending personalized lessons, gamification, and AI-powered feedback to make practice consistent, engaging and culturally relevant.

Project Details

  • Role: Product Designer & Researcher
  • Duration: 2 months
  • Team Size: 1
  • Tools & Tech: Figma, Google Sheets, React.js, AI music feedback frameworks
  • Category: Music Education, Adaptive Learning

Overview

Melodease combines adaptive lessons, gamified progress tracking, and AI-assisted feedback to create a motivating, personalized learning experience - with a focus on diverse cultural content and easy teacher-student loops.

Problem Statement

  • Students struggle to maintain consistent practice and motivation.
  • One-size-fits-all lessons lack personalization for different learners.
  • Most apps miss culturally relevant repertoire and real teacher feedback loops.

Competitive Analysis

Yousician Multi-instrument learners Gamified challenges Subscription Generic content Add cultural/localized content

Melodics Intermediate keyboardists Skill drills Subscription Dry, less engaging Add rewards, streaks

Competitors show gaps in Indian content, adaptive lessons, and instant feedback. Opportunity exists to integrate adaptive learning and gamified motivation.
